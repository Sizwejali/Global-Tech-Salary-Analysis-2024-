ğŸ” Key Insights
1. Job title strongly influences salary

Engineering-heavy roles (ML Engineers, Data Engineers) earn notably higher median salaries compared to analyst roles.

2. Experience level is the strongest predictor of pay

Executive-level (EX) compensation sharply spikes relative to all other levels.

Entry-level roles show tight salary ranges with minimal variance.

3. Company size affects the employment type, not just salary

Larger companies tend to rely more on full-time workers.
Smaller companies have higher proportions of contract and freelance roles.

4. COVID-19 (2020â€“2021) created a temporary dip

Your analysis reflects a measurable drop in median salaries attributed to global economic slowdown.

5. Remote work patterns differ by company size

Large companies: more zero-remote roles

Medium companies: most hybrid

Small companies: higher full-remote adoption

6. Geographic salary gaps remain high

North America salaries remain consistently higher across most roles and experience levels.
